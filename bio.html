A Quick Bio

I work in a high-energy physics lab where I’m researching the effectiveness of TPB-coated lightguide bars in a vacuum. Next year, I hope to work for an innovative technology company that is changing the way we interact with and perceive the world on a daily basis. In addition to sailing and programming, I enjoy exploring the ocean’s coastlines, landscape photography, and reading about 20th century history.
